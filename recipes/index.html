--- layout: page title: Recipes for Daria show-avatar : true --- In December 2022, my sister Daria shared that she has started eating a vegetarian diet. This list of my favorite vegan and vegetarian recipes is for her!

Vegan

  • butternut squash soup with crispy lentils (fall in a bowl, great with or without lentils) – Rainbow Plant Life
  • scallion pancakes (addictive, best fresh, feel free to sub ground ginger) – Bon Appetit
  • smacked cucumber with chili oil (crunchy, savory, spicy, YUM) – Pickled Plum
  • tofu piccata (crispy, lemony, capers) – From My Bowl
  • vegan thai curry vegetables (sub for whatever veggies, use a little too much soy sauce, throw in tofu if the mood strikes) – NY Times Cooking

Vegetarian

  • cucumber gazpacho (magnificent on a hot day) – Feating at Home
  • gnocchi al pomodoro (herby tomato sauce, cozy goodness) – Foodie Crush
  • kung pao tofu (gingery, a bit annoying to fry the tofu but so worth it, use black vinegar, feel free to sub scallion for leek) – China Sichuan Food
  • roasted acorn squash and gorgonzola pizza (great on store-bought dough, tastes totally decadent) – Smitten Kitchen
  • spicy and tangy broth with crispy rice (surprisingly easy and so comforting, adjust spice to preference) – Bon Appetit
